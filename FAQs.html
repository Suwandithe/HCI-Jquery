Starbucks FAQs Accordion

  • Where can I get a Starbucks card?

    Physical Starbucks Cards are available in at all Starbucks stores in your country or download Starbucks mobile app and add a Starbucks e-card onto your registered Starbucks account.

  • How do I use my Starbucks Card?

    You can use your Starbucks card or your Starbucks mobile app to purchase any item at all participating Starbucks store in your country, For example - food, beverage, retail coffee, Etc. The value in your Starbucks card cannot be changed for cash

  • How do I register my Starbucks card online?

    It is easy and only takes a few minutes at Starbucks official website or download the Starbucks mobile app on your phone. Visit the rewards page and create a new account. To create an account, click 'Create account', and then register your card. Starbucks will be sending existing Starbucks rewards member their 16-digit member number via email. Locate the email in your inbox and copy the 16-digit member number provided.
